Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you visit this site, standard technical information is collected automatically, including:

We use Google Analytics to understand general traffic patterns. Google Analytics does not provide NPC with personally identifiable information.

Information about how Google Analytics collects and processes data is available at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites (“How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services”). Google may modify or move its content from time to time.

If you contact NPC directly (such as by email), we receive only the information you choose to send. This website does not use forms, create accounts, or request or collect personal data.
